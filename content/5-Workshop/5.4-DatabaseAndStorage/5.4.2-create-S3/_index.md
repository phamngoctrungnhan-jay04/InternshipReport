In this section you will create S3 bucket to storage photos
1. Open the [Amazon S3](https://ap-southeast-1.console.aws.amazon.com/s3/get-started?region=ap-southeast-1)
2. Click **Create bucket**
![overview](/images/RDS/s1.png)
3. In create console, fill in name of bucket
![overview](/images/RDS/s2.png)
4. Then leave everything as default like picture
![overview](/images/RDS/s3.png)
5. Click **Create bucket** bottom
![overview](/images/RDS/s4.png)
